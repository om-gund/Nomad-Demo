The Barber

The Man Behind
the Nomad

Darius "D"
Master Barber & Founder
"Every man deserves to look sharp — whether you're in a penthouse or a parking lot."

Darius — known to his clients simply as D — spent years perfecting his craft in traditional barbershops before taking his skills on the road. What started as a calling became a movement: bringing elite-level grooming directly to the people, wherever they happen to be.

Nomad Barber Co. is more than a mobile barbershop. It's a philosophy. The belief that a great cut shouldn't require a commute, a wait, or settling for mediocre results. D arrives fully equipped — everything you'd find in the finest shops, minus the hassle.

Serving Chillicothe and surrounding areas, D has built a loyal following of clients who refuse to go back to the old way of doing things.

— D
